The Basics

01

Use a spreadsheet application for numerical data instead of a word processor

There have been many examples in past humanitarian responses of data more suitable to a spreadsheet being collected in a word processing document.

This means simple aggregation, operations and analysis cannot be completed without importing into a spreadsheet. Word tables can also be formatted in a way that does not copy well to spreadsheet meaning extra work before it can be used for analysis.
